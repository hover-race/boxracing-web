# Centerline generation

Vertices are read from the `1TARMAC_oval` mesh in `assets/glb/rcc-oval.glb`, then binned in polar coordinates (720 azimuth bins around the track centroid); each bin with enough samples becomes one centerline point at the mid-radius between the 10th and 90th percentile radii. Contiguous bins are chained into segments (gaps in bin index split at pit/straight breaks); segments shorter than 20 points are dropped, then each segment is smoothed and concatenated. The path is reversed, chunk tails before large gaps are trimmed to match 12 m marker spacing, and cumulative arc length is stored as normalized `u` in each point. `centerline.html` builds the line live via `centerlineFromTrack()`; `laps/track-centerline.json` is an optional export (`node scripts/generate-centerline.cjs`) or can be edited by hand. Gaps over 25 m between chunks are drawn as straight magenta bridge tubes in the viewer; cube labels every 12 m index the path.
